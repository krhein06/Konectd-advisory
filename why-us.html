What Sophisticated Buyers Want

  • Predictability: recurring or repeatable demand, contract structure, diversified customer base, and clear unit economics.
  • Scalability: professionalized operations, documented SOPs, KPI cadence, and a leadership bench beyond the owner.
  • Low diligence friction: clean financials, normalized EBITDA bridge, quality-of-revenue clarity, and organized documentation.
  • Growth optionality: organic levers and add-on acquisition adjacency (especially in fragmented markets with roll-up opportunity).
