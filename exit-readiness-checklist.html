Exit Readiness Checklist

A practical checklist to help you prepare for diligence, strengthen valuation drivers, and reduce the risk of late-stage retrades. Use this as a working document—check items off, add notes, and export/print for your team.

Download / Print / Track

1) Financial Readiness

Clean financials and clarity on earnings quality are the foundation of valuation.

2) Customer & Revenue Risk

Concentration and churn are major diligence themes that impact price and terms.

3) Operations & KPI Discipline

Repeatable delivery, documented processes, and clean KPIs reduce buyer uncertainty.

4) Team & Owner Dependence

Deals get stronger when leadership depth exists beyond the owner.

5) Legal, Compliance & Data Room

A clean, organized data room speeds diligence and reduces retrade leverage.
