Exit Readiness Checklist

Checklist Categories

Financial Readiness

Financial Clarity

  • Monthly P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow (24–36 months)
  • Documented add-backs (owner comp, one-time items, non-recurring)
  • Revenue breakdown by customer, segment, and service line
  • Gross margin by service line (and location if multi-site)
  • AR/AP aging + collections discipline
  • Tax returns aligned with reporting
Leadership Bench

Team & Org Depth

  • Org chart with role clarity and ownership of KPIs
  • Management cadence (weekly scorecards / metrics reviews)
  • Succession and owner transition plan
  • Comp plans + incentive structures documented
  • Hiring and training playbooks
  • Key-person risk mitigation
Operational Control

Operations & Delivery

  • Standard operating procedures for core workflows
  • Job costing discipline (where applicable)
  • Quality control process and feedback loop
  • Vendor list + key supplier dependencies
  • Capacity planning and utilization reporting
  • Safety/compliance documentation (as relevant)
Risk Reduction

Diligence Readiness

  • Entity documents, ownership, licenses, good standing
  • Customer contracts, MSAs, SOWs centralized
  • Insurance coverage summary + claims history
  • Real estate leases and equipment schedules
  • IP/software licenses + security posture (if relevant)
  • Disputes, litigation, warranty exposure documented
Narrative

Growth Story

  • Clear positioning: who you serve and why you win
  • Repeatable acquisition channels and sales process
  • Pricing strategy and margin expansion levers
  • Expansion roadmap (geo, service lines, cross-sell)
  • Competitive landscape summary
  • Case studies, testimonials, references
Execution

Process Planning

  • Confidentiality plan (who knows, when, and why)
  • Data room structure (financial, legal, HR, ops, customer)
  • Target buyer profile definition
  • Management presentation outline
  • Timeline and workload expectations
  • Negotiation priorities (price, structure, rollover, earn-outs)
