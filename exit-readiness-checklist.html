Exit Readiness Checklist

A practical framework to evaluate how prepared your company is for market engagement.

Financial Readiness

  • Three years of clean financial statements
  • Normalized EBITDA bridge
  • Customer concentration breakdown
  • Recurring vs project revenue clarity
  • Margin trend documentation

Operational Strength

  • SOPs documented and repeatable
  • Leadership depth beyond founder
  • KPI dashboards and reporting cadence
  • Diversified vendor relationships

Legal & Risk

  • Assignable contracts centralized
  • No unresolved litigation exposure
  • IP and licensing properly documented
