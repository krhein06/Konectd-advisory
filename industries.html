Repeat & Essential Services

HVAC, Plumbing & Electrical

Why buyers want it: non-discretionary demand, recurring service calls, membership plans, and strong local brand economics. Fragmentation creates roll-up opportunity and purchasing leverage.

What increases valuation: service mix > install mix, tech utilization, call center discipline, and add-on acquisition playbook.

Recurring demandFragmented marketAdd-on M&A ready

Restoration & Emergency Services

Why buyers want it: time-sensitive work, insurance-driven revenue, and repeat referrals from adjusters and property managers. Buyers like the defensibility of response infrastructure.

What increases valuation: referral diversity, cycle-time KPI rigor, and documented compliance/safety standards.

Insurance tailwindHigh urgencyOperational moat

Facilities Maintenance (Interior/Exterior)

Why buyers want it: contract-based recurring revenue and “mission-critical” uptime support for multi-site operators (retail, industrial, healthcare).

What increases valuation: contract structure, renewal rates, dispatch efficiency, and regional density.

Contracted revenueRoute densityMulti-location

Commercial Landscaping

Why buyers want it: sticky maintenance contracts and the ability to add higher-margin enhancements. Consolidation increases route density and labor utilization.

What increases valuation: customer retention, branch-level P&Ls, and dependable labor strategy.

Recurring contractsDensity economicsUpsell potential

Roofing & Exterior Residential Services

Why buyers want it: durable replacement cycle, storm-driven demand spikes, and repeatable sales/production systems.

What increases valuation: compliance, claims discipline, lead-gen diversification, and production controls that reduce rework.

Replacement cycleSystematizable opsScalable sales

Access & Security Systems

Why buyers want it: recurring monitoring/service, compliance-driven demand, and high switching costs for commercial clients.

What increases valuation: service contract base, response SLAs, and technician utilization.

Switching costsService revenueCompliance

Business & Professional Services

Managed IT & MSP

Why buyers want it: recurring managed services contracts, embedded client relationships, and consistent add-on demand (security, cloud, compliance).

What increases valuation: MRR quality, churn control, service desk maturity, and standardized tool stack.

MRRHigh retentionCross-sell

Accounting / Tax / CAAS

Why buyers want it: sticky client relationships, predictable annual workflows, and recurring advisory expansion.

What increases valuation: client mix, pricing discipline, staff leverage model, and tech-enabled delivery.

Recurring workflowsAdvisory expansionLow churn

Property & Association Management

Why buyers want it: recurring management fees, scale efficiencies, and strong retention when service quality is high.

What increases valuation: retention/renewals, standardized reporting, and diversified portfolio mix.

Recurring feesScale efficienciesRetention
