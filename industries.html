Industries We Focus On

We concentrate on industries where strategic buyers, private equity platforms, and family offices are actively deploying capital. These sectors demonstrate fragmentation, recurring revenue, durable demand, or consolidation opportunity.

Core Industry Verticals

Home & Commercial Services

Why buyers acquire: Fragmented markets, recurring service contracts, route density leverage, and strong free cash flow conversion.

Examples include HVAC, plumbing, electrical, restoration, landscaping, and specialty trades. These businesses often become add-on acquisitions for regional or national platforms.

Healthcare Services

Why buyers acquire: Aging demographics, reimbursement consistency, recurring patient demand, and roll-up opportunities.

Includes outpatient services, specialty clinics, behavioral health, and ancillary providers. Buyers seek scalable compliance frameworks and leadership depth.

Business Services

Why buyers acquire: Asset-light models, contractual revenue, and high margin predictability.

IT services, compliance providers, outsourced B2B services, and facility management firms are commonly targeted for platform expansion.

Distribution & Niche Manufacturing

Why buyers acquire: Specialized product niches, defensible supply chains, and purchasing power improvements through consolidation.

Strategic buyers seek proprietary SKUs, recurring reorder patterns, and differentiated sourcing.

Specialty Trade & Skilled Labor

Why buyers acquire: Licensing barriers, labor scarcity, and strong regional brand equity.

Businesses with certified technicians and recurring maintenance agreements often command premium multiples.
