Where Institutional Buyers Are Actively Deploying Capital

We focus on durable, service-oriented industries with recurring demand, fragmented ownership, and clear pathways to operational lift.

Home & Field Services

Recurring Maintenance Local Density Fragmented Market

HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, restoration, and route-based operators with essential demand characteristics.

Why buyers are active:
Private equity and strategic acquirers are building regional and national platforms through consolidation and operational standardization.

Why it's hot:
Predictable replacement cycles, strong pricing power, and the ability to professionalize dispatch, marketing, and reporting.

Diligence focus: job costing accuracy, technician productivity, membership penetration, and customer concentration.

B2B & Facility Services

Contract Revenue Multi-Year Agreements Retention Driven

Commercial cleaning, inspection, fire/life safety, compliance-based, and essential contracted services.

Why buyers are active:
Stable contracted revenue allows leverage and scalable multi-location growth.

Why it's hot:
Sticky customer bases and long-term contracts provide predictable EBITDA and financing support.

Diligence focus: renewal rates, labor structure, customer concentration thresholds.

Healthcare Services

Demographic Demand Recurring Utilization Platform Roll-Ups

Service-based healthcare operations benefiting from aging population and recurring utilization patterns.

Why buyers are active:
Platform strategies targeting add-on acquisitions and operational lift.

Why it's hot:
Strong demographic tailwinds and durable demand fundamentals.

Diligence focus: regulatory compliance, payer mix exposure, documentation depth.

Tech-Enabled Services

Automation High Retention Margin Expansion

Service businesses leveraging workflow systems, analytics, and recurring subscription overlays.

Why buyers are active:
Scalability and operating leverage through technology integration.

Why it's hot:
High retention rates and margin expansion through automation.

Diligence focus: churn analysis, acquisition cost trends, tech dependency.
